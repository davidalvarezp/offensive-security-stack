# Phishing Flow (Educational Walkthrough)

## Purpose
Describe phishing as a **process**, not a payload.

---

## Conceptual Phases
1. Preparation (lure design)
2. Delivery (communication channel)
3. Engagement (user interaction)
4. Outcome (credential or action)

---

## Why Phishing Works
- Trust exploitation
- Urgency cues
- Authority impersonation

---

## Defensive Measures
- User education
- Email authentication
- Behavioral detection

---

## Learning Outcome
Understand **human factors in security**.
