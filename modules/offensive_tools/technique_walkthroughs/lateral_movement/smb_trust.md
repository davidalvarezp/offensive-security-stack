# SMB Trust Abuse (Educational Walkthrough)

## Purpose
Explain how internal trust relationships can be misused.

---

## Concept Summary
SMB trust abuse leverages:
- Shared authentication contexts
- Implicit network trust
- Over-permissioned shares

---

## Risk Indicators
- Unexpected access paths
- Cross-segment authentication
- Abnormal file access patterns

---

## Defensive Controls
- Network segmentation
- Least privilege
- Access auditing

---

## Teaching Goal
Understand **why internal networks are not inherently safe**.
