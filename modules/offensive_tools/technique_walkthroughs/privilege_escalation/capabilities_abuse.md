# Capability Abuse (Educational Walkthrough)

## Purpose
Explain privilege escalation risks through fine-grained permissions.

---

## Concept Summary
Capabilities allow selective privileges, but:
- Misassignment expands attack surface
- Combinations create unintended power

---

## Risk Factors
- Over-granted capabilities
- Poor documentation
- Lack of review

---

## Defensive Measures
- Capability audits
- Minimal assignment
- Continuous review

---

## Teaching Objective
Understand **granular privilege does not equal safe privilege**.
