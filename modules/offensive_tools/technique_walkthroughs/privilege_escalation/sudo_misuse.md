# Sudo Misuse (Educational Walkthrough)

## Purpose
Explain how administrative delegation can introduce risk.

---

## Concept Overview
Sudo misuse occurs when:
- Rules are overly broad
- Command scope is unclear
- Context is ignored

---

## Risk Indicators
- Wildcard permissions
- Unrestricted command chaining
- Unexpected privilege paths

---

## Defensive Controls
- Principle of least privilege
- Explicit command definitions
- Regular review

---

## Educational Outcome
Understand **why controlled delegation requires discipline**.
