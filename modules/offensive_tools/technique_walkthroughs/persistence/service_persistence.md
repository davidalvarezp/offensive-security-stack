# Service-Based Persistence (Educational Walkthrough)

## Purpose
Describe how long-lived services can be misused conceptually.

---

## Concept Overview
Service persistence leverages:
- Automatic startup behavior
- High privilege contexts
- Limited visibility

---

## Defensive Indicators
- New or modified services
- Startup configuration drift
- Unexpected service behavior

---

## Defensive Controls
- Service allowlists
- Integrity monitoring
- Startup audits

---

## Learning Outcome
Recognize **boot-time trust boundaries**.
